Saint Paul's Parish Programs

Faith in Action: Serving our Community

Our Active Programs

Saint Paul's Parish Outreach runs a variety of programs aimed at improving the quality of life in our community. Whether it's helping those in need, providing healthcare services, or supporting youth leadership, we strive to create positive, lasting change. Here are some of the programs we are currently running:

Ongoing Programs

  • Clothing Reprocessing Program: Collecting and reprocessing old clothing for redistribution to those in need.
  • Volunteer Training Program: Teaching new volunteers the skills they need to serve the community effectively.
  • Community Food Drives: Regular food distribution to families and individuals facing food insecurity.
  • Faith Retreats: Spiritual retreats aimed at strengthening personal faith and offering moments of reflection and growth.
  • Youth Leadership Program: A mentorship program to help young people develop leadership skills and a sense of community responsibility.
  • Health and Wellness Clinics: Free medical and dental checkups for underserved community members.
  • Environmental Sustainability Initiatives: Organizing community clean-up drives and environmental education programs.
