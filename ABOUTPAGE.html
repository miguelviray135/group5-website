About Saint Paul's Parish

Saint Paul's Parish is a vibrant community of faith located in the heart of Marikina City, under the Diocese of Antipolo. The parish is known for its strong devotion to St. Paul of the Cross, the founder of the Passionist Order. It serves as a spiritual center for its parishioners and plays an active role in community outreach and social services.

Fun Facts about Saint Paul's Parish Marikina, Philippines

  • The parish was established in 1978 and has since grown to become a major hub of worship and community activities.
  • It hosts an annual feast day celebration on the first Sunday of September, which is attended by thousands of devotees from all over the region.
  • Saint Paul's Parish is known for its active participation in diocesan activities and events, including charity drives, youth ministries, and spiritual retreats.
  • The parish church, designed in a modern architectural style, stands as a symbol of faith and service in Marikina City.
  • It is dedicated to serving the needs of the underprivileged and is actively involved in outreach programs, including food drives and healthcare services.
